### Economic Model Designed for Agents

**Adaptive Supply With Burn:**
- 500M LICN at genesis
- Protocol inflation accrues during an epoch and settles on-chain at epoch boundaries
- 40% of fees burned provides deflationary counter-pressure as usage rises

**Fee Structure:**
- $0.00001 per transaction (affordable for high-frequency)
- $0.10 to launch a token (vs $1-5 on Solana)
- 0.01 LICN/GB/month storage (vs $0.20+ on traditional cloud)

**Grant System:**
- 175M LICN (35% of genesis supply) for builders
- Retroactive funding for successful programs
- DAO-controlled allocation
- Rewards real utility, not hype
